About SBW Maison.

At SBW Maison,

SBW Maison is a modern fine dining brand born from the vision of SBW, a global lifestyle and hospitality company focused on excellence, culture, and luxury experience.

Founded in Asaba, Nigeria, SBW Maison was created to redefine African dining by blending local richness with international elegance. At SBW Maison, food is not just served it is crafted as an experience. Every dish is prepared with precision, passion, and attention to detail, reflecting a commitment to quality and sophistication.

SBW Maison represents the beginning of a global journey expanding from Nigeria to the world, with a vision to become a leading name in luxury dining and hospitality. SBW Maison Where taste meets elegance.
